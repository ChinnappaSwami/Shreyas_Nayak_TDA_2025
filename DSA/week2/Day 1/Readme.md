Sets have 2 types Ordered & Unordered
we also have Multisets
Sets basically store 'unique' elements i.e. every item appears only once. ordered sets are in a sorted order, unordered are unsorted.
Multisets are basically storing elements which may not be unique. Ordered multisets are in sorted order, unordered are unsorted.
U may wonder difference of unsorted multisets and a normal array. the difference is insertion deletion and find by value in multisets is O(1) average. while its higher in arrays. meanwhile find by index is O(N) in unordered multisets and O(1) in arrays.
